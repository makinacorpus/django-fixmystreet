About this policy

This privacy policy sets out how fixmystreet.ca uses and protects any information that you give fixmystreet.ca when you use this website. fixmystreet.ca is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

What we collect

When you submit a report, or create an account with fixmystreet.ca, we collect the following information:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number

What we do with this information

If you submit a problem, we pass on your contact details, and details of the problem, to the municipality contact responsible for the area where you located the problem. When you submit a report or update, your name is displayed upon the site, but not your email address or phone number.

We will never give or sell your contact information to anyone else, unless we are obliged to by law.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
